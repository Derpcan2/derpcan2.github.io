The Ringed Seven

The Giants of Erlinion are integral to our history. They are gigantic towering humanoid beings who hold great power. They are not seen often, if ever, by most people. They are thus incredibly mysterious. There are many minor giants (our giant Faelyl falls into this category), but our history focuses on the importance of The Ringed Seven.

The reasoning for this title has actually been lost to time; its origin is heavily debated to be either a mistranslation of the ancient language or a reference to knowledge that is either lost or kept secret to this day. Personally, I subscribe to the latter.

The Ringed Seven are quite mysterious. Those who worship a certain giant typically aren't willing to divulge any information they've learned. All I have been able to gather (that has credible sources) amounts to the idea that The Ringed Seven participated in a war with each other for an incredibly long time. This war, known as The Ringed War, was devastating to the land and its inhabitants. The giants accumulated followers during the war, which eventually led to the creation of temples and communities that worship the giants today.

Plumaria

An unexpected development was the arrival of Gnosis, a Hand of Plumaria. Plumaria is one of The Ringed Seven. I don't have much information on her besides the idea that she was a great fighter who used the power of runes to control the skies. The Hand of Plumeria is the title belonging to the highest-ranking members of The Clear Skies Temple. The temple has a strict policy that restricts members from leaving, so seeing a Hand of Plumeria in Faylil was not only unexpected, it was bizarre.

Minor Giants

There are many Minor Giants throughout Erlinion's history. Most of these were significantly less powerful than The Ringed Seven and thus either stood by one of the seven during the war or protected small communities from the collateral damage of the battle. After the war, most either continued to serve one of The Seven, protect their community, or in rarer cases left their prior allegiance and found a home elsewhere.

Faelyl

Faelyl is a deeply important figure in our town's history. Of course, we derived our name from him, but there's more.
As the story goes:
"Once upon a time, Faylil was a small unnamed village. One day, an unknown evil summoned monsters to attack the town. The giant Faelyl appeared from the forest and defeated the monsters. The giant continued to protect the town...but one day, it suddenly left with a determined look on its face...it was never seen again."

This story occurred during The Ringed War. It is believed the monsters were servants of The Ringed Seven, either multiple groups fighting with each other or a single group that mistook the town as an enemy faction. He stayed and protected us for decades. He left around the beginning of the reign of King Fayne. Not even the King knows the reason for his departure or where he went. I hope one day he may return.
