1.1 The Meeting

My name is Solister I am the royal librarian of the town of Faylil. I am detailing these events as I believe they will be incredibly important to the future of our world.

The world of Erlinion is far and vast. People of all shapes and sizes live here. One fateful day...a special few met.

Sawyer and Fenwick approach the town of Faylil. The two are here to volunteer to aid the soldiers. They meet with one of the captains of the guard and are led to the soldiers quarters. There, they encounter "Graybeard", or at least, that is the name they are eventually given. "Graybeard" is a wizard found by the soldiers wandering around with no memory of who they are. They are given this name by one Slary Gloo Gullgruuf. One member of the duo of Slary and Bronz Faylil's famous heroes. The group awaited the soldiers but while doing so they encounter the Owlin, Twig in search of their missing sibling. The group eventually heads out to survey the forest neighboring Faylil. On the way the meet Gnosis, who is searching for knowledge of giants.

The group heads out to survey the forest. They encounter a normally harmless slime, which Sawyer makes an effort to show some affection, which the slime returns by rubbing against his leg. Then, it suddenly attacks the guard, and the group is forced to fight. After the battle, a strange fog fills the forest, and the group is separated from the captain.

Moving through the fog they eventually encounter the source. The duo of Shank and Dagger. They bicker and eventually introduce themselves as servants of "Lord Fargus." Before elaborating, a battle breaks out. The battle ends with Dagger (the bigger one) tied up. The group attempts to take them prisoner, but to their dismay, they escape.

They meet up with the rest of the soldiers and return back to Faylil, learning of a new enemy. The group splits into two groups. One takes lodge in a civilian's house and party while the other researches in the library.
