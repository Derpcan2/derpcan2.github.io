1.3 Flint and Steel

The team awakes from a nice sleep and heads downstairs, well, all except for Gnosis. They meet the king who tells them of a quest board. It has one quest on it:

"HELP NEEDED:
I NEED TO FREE MY DAD!!

Large party needed!!!

 I’ll pay 50g each!!

Details on the back!"

The team immediately set off on an adventure to the house of whoever set up the quest. They approach a small house, and see Shank and Dagger cornering a young man. The man is cowering with a sword drawn.

Upon seeing the team, Shank and Dagger flee, leaving the child. He rushes towards the team and tells them what happened. His name is Flint. His dad, coal, was exploring when he found a strange sword. He's disappeared ever since. Flint wants to save his dad.

Before they leave, though, he asks the party for a favor. He needs to fight them, to prove that they are strong enough to save his dad. The party denies the request before Fenwick agrees alone. The two have a one-on-one battle that isn't remotely close. Flint doesn't land a single blow. The battle is over before it starts. Fenwick doesn't have to break a sweat before walking away. The party promises that they'll save his dad, but Flint needs to remain here.

Tears in his eyes Flint runs ahead of the group trying to save to his father. The group chases after him.

They follow him upon a mountain path before reaching a dangerous rock environment. They catch up to Flint before he is stopped by a Tiefling assassin holding a knife to his throat. The party defeats the assassin and makes them flee. Before they leave they hurt Flint badly. The party urges Flint to stay down or they'll make him. He continues trying to get up before he is quickly knocked unconscious.

They bring him up the mountain protecting his unconscious body. Reaching the top of the mountain. There they see Coal. He turns towards them and laughs.

"So I assume my pathetic son sent you to save me?" He continues to insult Flint. Saying that the only reason that this team will die is because Flint burdened them with his existence. The party immediately draws their weapons. Sawyer steps forward, saying that he has no patience for dead beat dads.

The party do battle with Flints dad before finally knocking the sword out of his hands. The sword flies out and the impact of the sword piercing the ground wakes Flint. He rushes over to his dad who looks the team up and down.

"You guys did it! Dad we can go home."

"No...I'm sorry Flint that blade took too much out of me. I don't have much longer."

The team immediately tries to take the sword, but Coal warns that touching it will corrupt and to stay away. The team is determined, though, and a few of them decide to all grab onto the sword and try to handle it as a team. However, the power of the sword is overwhelming. Two of them fall to the ground, unconscious. Three remain grabbing on to the sword: Sawyer, Slary and Bronz. Sawyer suddenly pulls the sword to himself, his eyes changing shade. Slary and Bronz support each other and break free of the blade.

Sawyer looks at the rest of the team, laughs, puts the sword on his back, and pulls out his quarterstaff. Suddenly a sharp piercing blade extends from the end of the staff. He rushes towards the party fighting viciously. The team fights back but not before he lands a tough blow on Twig. They knock the sword away from him, and he falls unconscious. They make sure he's safe before returning to the issue at hand: The son who's losing his father.

They proceed to try destroying the blade with their weapons before honing in on the crystal in the middle of the sword. Maybe, just maybe, this can save him. Hammers and Blades smash the crystal before they eventually shatter it. They think they've won when suddenly the blade begins to float, dark power shooting out of it before it shoots off into the distance. Milly, thinking fast, extends a thorny whip to try and pull the sword back to the ground, but the sword breaks the whip before darting off.

Their eyes return to Coal and Flint.

“You are a brilliant young man. Despite your fear, you still went to save me…I couldn’t have asked a better son…”

“Dad! No no please don’t do this, we can go home, we can go back to normal no...I cant let you, I need to show you that I’m-”

“Son...keep being you...thats all you need to do to make me proud...I love you...”

Flint pulls his dad in close before he suddenly fades away...Flint stays frozen on the ground looking at his hands.” He screams into the heavens.

“No...no...why...WHYYYYYY.”

The team stands silent, unsure what to say. Flint looks at them and throws a bag of money on the ground before looking away again.

“Leave me alone...you finished your job. Take your reward.”

Some of the team takes the money while others leave it. They leave the boy to himself.

On the way home, Sawyer wakes up. He asks if they saved the father. The team quietly and painfully details what happened. Sawyer is immediately frustrated and upset. They had failed. It wasn't fair, he was too weak and he hurt his team. He begins to spiral before Slary cuts through.

"We did what we would could...I wish we could've saved him too, but you can't always save everyone. We didn't do anything wrong, and we can't blame ourselves."

The words resonate with the whole group. Somewhat lightening the mood. However, the journey home is still plagued with silence.

They reach the town gates to an unexpected site. A festival with fireworks lit up the town.

A banner strung on the gate read
"Welcome home, heroes of Faylil."

But it was hard to feel like heroes right now.

They humored the town and engaged in festivities before returning to the Faylil Inn and escaping to sleep.

1 MONTH LATER:
A young man marches up a rocky mountain pass mumbling to himself...

"He's gone...because of me...because I wasn't fast enough."

"I wasn't strong enough."

"Those two...they knew about the sword...did they make it?"

"And they knew...they knew that group..."

"If the group knew them...they knew they were evil....and allowed them to live?"

“Those kinds of people are disgusting...they’re a stain on this earth...”

“I need to be stronger...I need to be able to destroy them...even if I have to become one of them to do it”

Suddenly, he approaches what he's been looking for...a purple-hilted blade with a hole where a crystal should be, stabbed into the ground. He grabs hold of the sword and raises it high. A twisted grimace crosses his face.

“They took everything from me...because I was a coward...I couldn’t do anything...but not anymore....I’ll make them pay...”
