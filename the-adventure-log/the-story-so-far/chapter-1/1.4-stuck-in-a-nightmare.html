1.4 Stuck in a Nightmare

Waking up, the team heads downstairs, the previous day's failure weighing over their heads. They look over to the quest board and spot two quests pinned up. This, at the very least, distracts the team from the events of yesterday. They read two quests. One seems to be asking for help with a "business meeting" and the other is about saving someone who was trapped in a nightmare. The team decides on the latter and sets off.

They arrive at the specified location, which ends up being a small home. Suddenly a girl bursts out of the house. The energetic lady introduces herself as Mara. She dashes into her home and the team follows behind.

They enter a bedroom to see a man lying on the bed, out cold. Mara introduces the man as her brother Mufa. She explains that the two are adventures always seeking treasure and excitement. One day the two found an invitation to "the kingdom of dreams". A few days later they arrive at the location, but Mara saw nothing. Mufa on the other hand appeared to have seen an entire kingdom. In her words

"It was strange, though, when we eventually “found” it, I didn't see a thing. He ran towards what he said were the castle gates, but I just saw him running toward...well, nothing but more grass. But he started talking to people that I couldn’t see, and eventually, he went...underground I guess? He kinda just phased into the floor. Eventually, he came back out, but he was injured. I ran over to him and he fell asleep. He’s been like this ever since he’ll occasionally sleep talk and whatever he’s dreaming about...it doesn’t sound pleasant.”

She then tells the team how she found a spell that would allow her to go into his dreams and save him. She was scared of doing this alone, so she put out a quest. The spell offers her protection from any elements in there but not anyone else, so she cautions them to be wary.

She summons a portal and they all hop in. Graybeard is last and sees two figures entering the house as they jump in.

They enter the world of dreams to find a strange world made up of different landmasses and textures. The team sees a strange cloud and enters it, and receives a piece of a blueprint and a letter. The letter reads:

"I do not know how much time will pass before this is found, but I leave the assembly of this weapon to others...My time for adventuring has passed, but I feel that whoever finds this...their time may just be beginning.  Collect the others, Hero."

It's signed by some kind of G name, but it aged too much to read the rest. Slary takes important note of the letter and blueprint, and decides that he wants to attempt to create this weapon. The team then follows the path lead out before them.

They eventually reach an impass when the find themselves faced with a large gap. They eventually make their way across in a multitude of different ways before continuing on.

They enter a sinister crimson red area. The area leads them to a dungeon. Mara says that she remembers a dungeon like this, but it feels off. The floor is shifting and appears to be made of bricks and stone from different places. Even the lighting on the materials is inconsistent.

The group is exploring when suddenly they hear a familiar voice. They rush forward to see Flint's father calling out for help. The rush towards him, but he suddenly fades away and the group is trapped in a room.

They solve a puzzle in the room and manage to escape and are greeted by the face of Mufa cowering on the floor. But in front of him sits a monstrous creature. Like a pile of sludge, it slithers across the ground, and stares blankly with cold hate-filled eyes. The crew prepares to fight it when suddenly it changes shape and the same feelings envelope all of them:

It morphs into a man with a striped mask…he’s holding something…no he’s taking important to you...he betrayed you...you thought someone finally loved you...He takes off his mask and you see...nothing...his face is blurry...who is he...where are you...who is anyone...why can't you remember..why can't you recognize anything... what's happening...then he gestures all around you and the world shifts...you find yourself cowering in fear in a small store...the store is in ruins...your sister she's running into the streets...no...she's running towards her death...she runs into a bloodbath…the village...it's being destroyed...they invaded...the tribes should've been able to live in peace...your parents...they’re running...but they don’t escape…pain...so many different kinds of pain...why should you keep going...why should you keep fighting...what did the world do for you but deliver you pain.

Mara realizes what's happening, but it's too late. The monster is merging the group's traumatic memories and experiences together and forcing them to endure that pain.

The group is forced to their knees, but one by one some of them manage to stand. They manage to shake away the fear that is encompassing them and their comrades. They push through the pain and slaughter the nightmare.

It crumbles into dust, and what's left is Mufa. Mara runs over but suddenly Shank and Dagger appear! They grab Mufa's weak body.

“HAHA! You fool! Thanks for solving all the puzzles for us! A little bit of door smashing later and our plan was in motion”

“So I hope you're a big napper!”

“Cuz you’re about to be trapped by shank and dagger!!!”

With a snap of his finger, the three disappear, and suddenly, the dream begins to crumble. The group is in a disastrous state, but theres no time for sorrow. They rush out of the dungeon before reaching the gap. Sawyer grabs a potion and drinks it, growing massive. He then carries everyone across the gap to their freedom.

The crew bursts out of the dream. Landing on the wooden floor of the house. They celebrate for a second before realizing what had happened. They had failed to save Mufa. Mara panics and dejectedly hands the team the gold she had promised them. Sawyer suddenly snaps at her before leaving in a huff. The team hurries out, but not before Mara tells them that she will do some research and put up another quest as soon as shes ready to go save her brother.

The team returns home, once again silent. While not as great of a loss as the previous day, it was still a loss. Back to back, those losses really hurt. The head to the inn.

They sit and talk to each other in small groups. Some alone. Hoping that whatever adventure comes next will go better.
