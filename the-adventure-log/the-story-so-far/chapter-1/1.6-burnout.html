1.6 Burnout

It's been a fortnight since the team's previous adventure. The quest board seemingly remained barren. Bored, tired, and dejected, the party sluggishly makes their way down the stairs of the inn to be once again greeted by an empty board. Ready to return to their lodging, the party's attention is suddenly focused on a commotion taking place just outside of the inn. They head outside to see a crowd of people surrounding someone. After making their way to the center of the crowd, they come face-to-face with the Flame Knight. The crowd roars and cheers over his arrival as the heroes stand confused, unaware of the reason for the excitement. It isn't too long before they realize the cause of the commotion.

The knight stands proudly, holding up a quest in his hands. People cheer him on as he walks towards the town gates. The team runs up to him and attempts to convince him to let them join him on the quest. He declines and continues walking.

He's interrupted by Mara who crashes into him as she darts into town. She apologies and says that she needs to find the people who took her quest and the knight tells her that she's found them. She's confused before he holds the quest in his hand, revealing it to be the one she had put up. She's shocked and simultaneously notices the party behind the knight.

She says that she thought there was only one team of heroes in Faylil. The knight says that he's new here, and he doesn't fail, unlike the other heroes here.

She says that she won't allow him to continue unless he lets the other heroes join them on the quest. He begrudgingly agrees. Mara casts a spell that creates a beacon in Mufa's direction. The team sets off into the forest.

On the way to the beacon, a slime jumps in front of the crew. Mara expresses excitement in seeing the slime just before the flame knight steps on it, killing it instantly. This immediately angers Milly who yells at the flame knight. The knight questions why the party bothers with piles of goo or other wastes of time when real people need saving. He continues walking before the party can argue more.

Eventually, they get deeper into the woods. While looking for the path forward, they are suddenly ambushed by the same assassin who kidnapped Flint. She states that they won't get away this time when suddenly...she's cut down. In a series of brutal slashes the Flame Knight tears through her and leaves her on the ground.

"Pathetic.... let's go. If we're lucky, the other two tieflings will join her."

The party runs up to the body and attempts to stabilize it.

"Why are you wasting your time saving your enemy? Heroes do what they must to save the people in trouble. "

Sawyer retorts, saying that it seems like he's just using the excuse of being a hero to do what he wants.

The knight is angered and states that if he has to tear down the team to save Mufa he will. But before he does he lets them in on a secret: The origin of his name...
"It's quite simple....Im just the sum of my parts...Steel...and Flint."

In a burst of flame, he reveals himself to be Flint, under the influence of the sword of darkness. He then battles the team, but is overpowered.

He attempts to leave, but Sawyer gets in his way and attempts to knock him out. He's left on the ground, and to prevent his escape Twig places the immovable rod onto him. The party deliberates on what to do. They eventually come to the conclusion that they need to cut off his arm.

Multiple members try different ways to sever the arm, but the sword's power keeps healing it. Eventually though, Aurum manages to get a slice in while its trying to heal and slices the arm clean off. The sword is left on the ground.

The team tries to destroy it once again, but come up short. The sword forms a hard shell. Slary manages to destroy the shell, but the sword immediately tries to latch onto him. It begins slowly crawling up his arm, but the party manages to separate him from it in time.

The issue then becomes: what to do with the sword. Salary utilizes his wand of secrets and discovers what appears to be a secret village of fey-like people. The party doesn't feel comfortable dropping the sword off on this unsuspecting village. They decided to make note of the place and come back later. Eventually, sing some cloth from the flame knight's scarf, the party manages to wrap up the sword to safely transport it.

The party manages to take the flame knight armor off of Flint, and Milly uses a thorn whip to transport it without being burned.
The team continues on deeper into the forest.

They manage to make their way to Shank and Dagger. The pair is standing with an unconscious Mufa...and a strange machine.

They greet the party smugly as Shank explains what the machine is. When Shank attempted to steal the blueprints during their previous encounter, he managed to memorize the blueprints and build an approximation of the device, and use Mufa as a battery for nightmare energy.

He points the machine in the party's direction and blasts them with it.

Suddenly the party is on a pirate ship...familiar to one of them. Sawyer immediately freezes up. Mara tries to use a spell to transport them out, but something is preventing the spell from working. Suddenly pirate apparitions swarm the ship. The party fights them off before the spell goes off. This time they end up in the ruins of a burning village...Fenwick is enraged. This happens several times, a library familiar to Gnosis, a world that encapsulates a feeling Graybeard knows all too well, a one sided war that Bronz recognizes, a forest home to Milly, the solider's quarters where Aurum spent years locked up and Twig first met humanity, and The town of Faylil under the control of fargus: a day Slary almost failed.

After traversing the multiple nightmares the party breaks out and lands back in reality. Shank and Dagger stand stunned as the machine collapses. They stand ready to fight when they are suddenly, seemingly involuntarily, taken away. The light of them reappearing can be seen off in the distance. Mara grabs hold of Flint and Mufa and takes them back to town, urging the party to chase after Shank and Dagger.

The party continues the chase, but stumble upon an unexpected sight...the skeleton of a giant.
