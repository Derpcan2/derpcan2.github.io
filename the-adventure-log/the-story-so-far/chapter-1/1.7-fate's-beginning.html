1.7 Fate's Beginning

The party is face to face with the skeleton of a giant. It's covered in dirt and stone that leave in a cave like shape. Slary immediately goes to investigate the skeleton's tooth in the hopes of detaching it and crafting it into a shield. While investigating it, he's able to deduce that not only has this been here for at least several decades, but the skeleton appears to have allowed itself to be killed. It is covered head to toe in scratches that imply the beast stood still as something carved into it.

The titans jaw is gaped open acting as the entrance to a cave. The party ventures into find a chest sitting in the middle of the path. They once again find a blueprint, and while not in the chest, they somehow obtain several items, including a bag of holding and a driftglobe.

They make their way forward to find Shank and Dagger. Suddenly the Flame Knight armor begins shaking and flies away. Slary bribes Dagger with a banana in order to get him to spill where they are. Shank attempts to stop him, but Dagger overpowers him and tells the party that they are currently in the remains of the giant Faelyl.

Suddenly their conversation is interrupted by a voice from the corner of the room.

"Shank and Dagger, I can see that...well one of you is ready to resort to violence, and the other...I'm not sure. Either way, surely this isn't how we treat guests."

The man floats towards the party.

"My apologies. These two must have caused you a lot of trouble on my behalf. We’re practically family, so they’ve always been quite overprotective of me.”

“Ah, wait, I’ve forgotten to introduce myself. My name is Geralt...Geralt Fargus...”

The party immediately prepares to fight upon hearing that name and Fargus senses this.

“I know that name most likely doesn’t have the most pleasant connotations in your head, and with how fickle fate can be I imagine this is its way of course correcting...Oh, excuse me, I don’t mean to confuse you, essentially. I understand that this meeting surely will devolve into a battle, but please humor me for a second.”

He pulls out...a cake?

"You see before I became what I am today.I always wanted to be a chef. That’s why I prepared a cake for all of you. Oh and Milly! I prepared a vegan cake for you as well. I didn’t know what your diet was, but I assumed you only ate vegan just to be safe.”

This gesture is scrutinized heavily by the whole team. Graybeard warns that they don't think it is safe to eat it.

Sawyer eventually caves and tries the cake. He eats it, and it tastes rather mediocre. Clearly, there's nothing actually wrong with it, or at least...it's not harmful.

Geralt thanks the party for humoring him when suddenly his smile fades.

"Thank you. I greatly appreciate you all. Shank and Dagger rise. Now...face the villain of your story."

He's clocked in shadow and splits into three. In a harsh battle, he managed to knock much of the team down, and when they got back up, he would knock them down again. They finally manage to take down one of the shadow clones. Fargus stops fighting once they do.

“Hmm....A pleasant surprise. Has fate finally found new heroes? Or is it simply using you to get back on track...Only time will tell. Apologies for leaving in this manner...you must be quite tired of it by now...We will meet again. Goodbye."

He snaps his fingers and he teleports away. Normally, the party is upset by this, but considering the shape they are in, they don't mind it this time. The party tiredly makes their way back to Faylil.
When they arrive they are greeted by Mara who tells thanks them for everything.

The party asks her if she's seen the flame knight's armor, and she sighs and points behind her. The soldier's quarters have a flaming hole in its side.

Mara explains that the armor suddenly flew into town and burst through the wall. Freeing Flint who walked out wearing the armor and walked away. He left behind the gauntlet he no longer needed and a note.

“Everyone...Sorry if this is a little hard to read. I was...right-handed. I know your first instinct is probably to, first be confused on how I got the armor back and second to be angry with me. So I’ll do my best to explain myself...I think its pretty clear why I sought out that damned sword. I was weak, I always had to rely on others…I thought the sword would fix me, but in the end...I just relied on something else like I always did. When I woke in my cell, I heard screaming in my head. It was the sword...it’s trying to get me to come back and get it...I won’t. But I figured that if the sword was still bonded to me...perhaps the armor was too. I made a gamble, and suddenly, the armor flew onto me...sorry for the hole in the wall. I’m going to find out how to destroy that thing. No matter what it takes, no one should become like the flame knight...like me...no one should have to deal with that thing.

I'm sorry,
Flint

Sawyer takes the gauntlet.

The party returns home after what was one of the most eventful days of their lives. They make the decision to keep the death of the giant Faelyl secret. With that they head off to sleep...questions ringing in their minds. What was Fargus’ goal? What did he mean by fate course correcting...These thoughts fill their mind as they drift off to sleep.
